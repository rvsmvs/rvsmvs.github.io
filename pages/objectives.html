Learning Objectives

Knowledge

  • Understand core malware analysis methodologies, describe common malware architectural patterns, and characterize essential technical features, including encryption, persistence mechanisms, and network and command-and-control (C2)
  • Gain insight into Threat Intelligence principles, including Indicators of Compromise (IoCs), Tactics–Techniques–Procedures (TTPs), and the MITRE ATT&CK framework.

Competence

  • Design and maintain an isolated laboratory environment for static and dynamic malware analysis, adhering to security and ethical best practices.
  • Translate analysis results into actionable recommendations for detection, incident response, and preventive controls.
  • Compare and validate analytical findings against established threat reports and vendor intelligence..

Skills

  • Identify and document IoCs and map TTPs to MITRE ATT&CK.
  • Produce clear, rigorous technical reports and visualizations that communicate the malware life cycle and end-to-end attack flow.
