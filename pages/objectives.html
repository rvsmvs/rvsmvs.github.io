Learning Objectives

Knowledge

  • Understand core malware analysis methods, ransomware architecture, and key technical characteristics (encryption, persistence, and network/C2 communication).
  • Gain insight into Threat Intelligence principles, including Indicators of Compromise (IoCs), Tactics–Techniques–Procedures (TTPs), and the MITRE ATT&CK framework.

Competence

  • Set up an isolated lab for static and dynamic malware analysis.
  • Translate analysis results into concrete recommendations for detection, response, and prevention.
  • Compare and validate findings against established threat reports.

Skills

  • Identify and document IoCs and map TTPs to MITRE ATT&CK.
  • Produce clear technical reports and visualizations of the ransomware lifecycle and attack flow.
